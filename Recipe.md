take 3/4 cup All Purpose Flour
3 spoons Curd
1/2 spoon Sugar
1/2 spoon Salt
3/4 spoon Baking Powder
1/2 spoon Baking Soda
mix them all and make a soft dough.
make medium size ball and roll them .
then put it on the pan and heat till bubbles come on it.
then spread pizza sauce on it. 
and grate the cheese as much as you like.
then take another pan greese it with oil.
then put the pizza on it 
heat the pizza till the cheese melts.
then cut it in four slices and serve it.
